Volunteering at the
Waterford Public Library


By Lorelei Peck
The Waterford Library can always
use more volunteers for their summer reading program!
Volunteering is a great and fun way to help the community!
When you volunteer, you can either:
  • Help with one of their many programs
  • Help more people sign up for the summer reading program
  • or help little kids with their projects!

It's also a great way to meet new friends!


Here is a link to the Library website!
At that website, you can see some of the many programs coming up!

To sign up for volunteering, you can go to the library
and talk to either the front desk or the teen librarian!
